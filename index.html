Rules of the game

Each participant has a robot (it is your pawn), the objective is to keep their robot alive, to do so, you have to answer some questions and some challenges. There is a modular life point system, so you can choose as much as you want. Each player has a certain number of lives at the beginning of the game, itâ€™s represented by a stack. When you answer a question wrong you lose one of them. You can win them with certain events on the board, but be careful you can lose some too. When you finish the board, you go back to the beginning of it. The last robot alive wins the game !
